Akira Toriyama

Japanese mangaka and character designer

Here's a time line Akira Toriyama life:

  • 1955 - April 5, Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture, Japan
  • 1973 - He entered the Industrial High School, immediately after he was hired by an advertising company in Nagoya.
  • 1978 - he made his debut as a cartoonist at a young age.
  • 1981 - Thanks to it, he received the Manga Award, awarded by the publisher Shōgakukan.
  • 1982 - Marries nachi mikami
  • 1987 - Birth of his first son Toriyama Sasuke
  • 1990 - Birth of his daughter Toriyama Kikka
  • 1995 - His life as a mangaka began with a publication in Shūeisha's Shonen Jump. Later he would create many other stories for that magazine and later collected in volumes. Toriyama became immensely popular in Japan and abroad thanks to his work Dragon Ball.
  • 2009 - In Japan, however, he remains a public figure and still lives in Aichi with his wife and children, where his fame has caused him some trouble.

"Akira Toriyama today is practically retired from the field, although he is still present in the direction, production and scriptwriting of the most important Dragon Ball projects."
